Welcome to RL 6Mans
The only competitive PUG platform for Rocket League

What is 6Mans?

6Mans is a competitive pick-up game (PUG) system run through Discord where you compete against people of the same skill in order to rise through the ranks and join the Professionals in the Pro League!

Why should I play?

If you're looking to go take your game to the next level, The 6Mans system is proven to be a more competitive environment compared to ranked due to the introduction of voice communication with your teammates.

How do I play?

To begin playing, hit the Play button on the left, or the button below.

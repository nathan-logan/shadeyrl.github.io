Start your Journey
Follow the instructions below

Step 1: Join Discord

If you do not have Discord installed, follow this link

Once you have Discord installed, Click this link to head to our Discord servers page.

Join the Discord server for the region in which you live.

Note: You are only allowed to play in one(1) region, this is the region where you sign up.

Step 2: Get your rank

Once you have joined the Discord head to the #info channel and follow the instructions there.

You will be assigned a rank based on your Rocket League 3s rank (Standard and Solo Standard).

If you are unsure on some things, read the #faq channel.

If you are still hesitant, tag a moderator in the help chat for more assistance.

Step 3: Game on!

Lastly, head into your rank channel and type !q to enter a queue and !leave to leave one. This channel should look like one of these.

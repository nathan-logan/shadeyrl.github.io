About 6Mans

6Mans is a free to play competitive match making system run entirely through Discord for the infamous car soccer game, Rocket League. You are placed into a rank where you compete with players of a similar skill level, when you reach a certain threshold on the leaderboards, you are promoted. As you rank up the skill level increases, encouraging you to improve in order to keep progressing.

GET STARTED

Step 1: Register

To play in 6Mans in the FACEIT Hub, you require a FACEIT Account

Keep in mind you won't be able to change your name after you create your account unless you spend 1000 FP in the shop, so make it a recognizable nickname.







ADD SOMETHING ELSE HERE

Step 2: Add Game

To compete in the 6Mans FACEIT Hubs you must connect Rocket League to your FACEIT account by following these steps:

1. Click Rocket League

2. Click register and sign in with your Steam/Xbox account
(Remember to set your Steam acccount to public before signing up!)

3. Click connect.




Step 3: Join a Hub

All new accounts are assigned a FACEIT level based on their Rocket League rank (the distribution can be found here) Each division has a skill requirement to join.

Levels 1-5 can join Divison 5
Levels 6-7 can join Divison 4
Levels 8-9 can join Divison 3
Levels 10 can join Divison 2
Pro Players are invited to Divison 1

As you play games your FACEIT level will go up or down depending on how you do.

Step 4: Join the Discord

Download and install Discord. It is required for you to be in voice for your matches.

Once you have downloaded and installed Discord, join the server relative to your region by clicking the links below:

European Players
North American Players
Oceaniac Players

Step 5: Connect

Link your Discord account to your FACEIT account to merge them into the 6Mans Pro League system.


Once completed you can msg the 6MansBot in Discord with the message !verify to check your accounts are linked.

Step 6: Read the Rules

6Mans Pro League strives to provide the best competitive Rocket League environment for our players, we have clear guidelines on what not to do and how you will be punished for your actions.

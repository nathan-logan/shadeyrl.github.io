SOCIALS

Hubs for every skill level.

Each region has their own set of hubs for each skill level. When you sign up to FACEIT with your steam account you are given a FACEIT level representing the rank you are currently at in-game. These are displayed in the image to the left. Once you receive that rank you will be eligible to play in the hubs that correspond to your skill level. There you can start to compete with people of similar skill.


Level up as you play.

As you play games in the 6Mans Pro League Hubs you gain FACEIT ELO. This it the number that determines what level you are and which hubs you are eligible to compete in. Once you reach the threshold for the hub above you are able to join that hub and begin queueing.




Desktop client.

The FACEIT Desktop client has play competitions, see who's online, chat with your team and catch up with your favourite pros and organisers using the new navigation. You can chat with friends, party members, team mates and even hub communities using the new chat. See all the useful notifications including competition invites, friend requests and hub mentions. And finally, chat play and compete in the community hubs!
